Changing Logo File and Header Image

If you would like to change the header of your Omeka site to a custom logo file, you can! Go to Appearance, then Customize theme. There you can upload custom files to Omeka to serve as your header. Remember,

  • Files have a max width of 500 pixels.
  • Files cannot be larger than 300kB.
Other than those two restrictions, you should be able to use any image you want.
