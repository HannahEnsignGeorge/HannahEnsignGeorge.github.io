Changing Navigation on Your Omeka Site

Don't like the way Omeka automatically organizes your site navigation? Change it to the one you want! Need to add more pages to cover information about your team or background on the project? Add more pages!

  1. Go to Appearance-> Navigation-> Select links and move them around to find the best arrangement
  2. Go to Appearance-> Navigation-> check boxes to customize available headings
  3. Go to Appearance-> Navigation-> add a new page-> customize URL
